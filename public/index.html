How to play

The initial word is given by the computer.
Players take turns to make a word by reusing one or more letters from the previous word. A player gets 10 points for every letter in the fragment that is reused.
The new word cannot be a subset of the previous word. A player must add at least one letter during their turn. No points are awarded for the new letters added
Only contiguous letters are taken into account for scoring. When more than one fragment is reused, the longer one will be considered for scoring. In this example, B and L do not fetch any points.
Letters may be “pinned” or “banned” during a turn. A letter that is pinned must compulsorily be used in the next word (not necessarily in the same position). A letter that is banned cannot be used in the next word. Each player can only initiate 3 challenges (pin or ban) in a game. Pin and Ban cannot be used in the same turn.

In this example, letter B is pinned in the 4th turn and letter I is banned in the 5th turn. There are no extra points for pinned or banned letters

The game ends when each player has played 5 turns each. The player with the highest score wins.

The final score is the points earned by the player in all turns combined.
